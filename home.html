Welcome to Tic Tac Toe

About the Game

Tic Tac Toe is a classic game played between two players. Mark your squares, strategize, and aim for three in a row to win!

Whether you're playing for fun or to challenge your friends, this game offers an exciting and timeless experience.

Preview of the Game

Key Features

  • Two-player mode: Red vs. Blue
  • Responsive game board
  • Real-time player turns display
  • Game history and score tracking
  • Visually appealing design
